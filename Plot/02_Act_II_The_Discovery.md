# Act II: The Discovery

- **Act's Objective:** Molly discovers the truth about the outside world and the approaching terraforming crisis.
- **Key Points:**
    - Molly's growing technical skills allow her to bypass lab security systems.
    - **The Discovery:** She finds Elias's hidden files revealing: 1) His ongoing communication with settlements, 2) The Accord's terraforming timeline (6-12 months), 3) Settlement death rates from environmental poisoning, 4) Her own neural monitoring data labeled "Molly's Song."
    - Molly realizes Elias has been lying about the settlements being "savage" and that people are dying while he does nothing.
    - She makes contact with the outside world and meets Kael through a hacked communication system.
- **Act's Climax:** Molly escapes the lab to save the settlements, believing she and Kael can find a solution before time runs out.
