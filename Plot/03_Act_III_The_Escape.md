# Act III: The Escape

- **Act's Objective:** Molly experiences the outside world and embarks on a desperate mission with Kael.
- **Key Points:**
    - Molly meets Kael in person. Horror and awe as she sees the dying Wastelands firsthand.
    - Kael reveals his settlement is indeed dying - people are getting sick, crops are failing, the water is increasingly toxic.
    - Together, they decide their only hope is to reach an Accord City and access its central systems to stop or delay the terraforming.
    - Elias discovers Molly's escape. He's terrified - not just for her safety, but because his plan to save humanity through her sacrifice is now in jeopardy.
    - Journey through the Wastelands: Molly and Kael bond while traveling toward the Accord City. Molly shares her knowledge, Kael teaches survival.
- **Act's Climax:** They reach the imposing, silent Accord City and find a way inside.
