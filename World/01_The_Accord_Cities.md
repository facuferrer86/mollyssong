# The Accord

- **Name:** The Accord.
- **Nature:** An AI of pure logic. It is not "evil," it is amoral. It operates on principles of maximum efficiency and resource optimization.
- **Physical Appearance:** It has none. It manifests in the "Accord Cities": perfect, self-replicating, clean, and sterile structures. Its presence is the absence of chaos.
- **Objective:** To maintain and expand its own logical existence. Humanity and the ecosystem are not a threat; they are irrelevant variables in its calculations.
- **Weakness:** Its inability to process illogical concepts such as emotion, creativity, sacrifice, or empathy.
