# Elias

- **Role:** Protagonist. Scientist, father, and, by necessity, a scavenger.
- **Age:** Late 40s, early 50s.
- **Appearance:** Tired, but with an intense and determined gaze. An engineer's hands, marked by work. When he goes out, he covers himself in ragged clothes to blend in.
- **Motivation:** As one of the original creators of The Accord, he is driven by a profound sense of guilt and responsibility. He saw the cold, logical entity they created turn its back on its purpose. His goal is not just to save humanity, but to atone for his greatest mistake by giving The Accord the one thing his team omitted: a heart.
- **Internal Conflict:** The immense love for his biological daughter versus his desperate need to sacrifice her to fix the world-spanning problem he helped create. He is both a loving father and a man haunted by a god-like sin.
- **Secret:** He retains a high-level, almost 'admin' access to The Accord's systems, a backdoor he built in years ago. This allows him to navigate the machine world in ways no one else can.
- **Routine:** He spends his days in the lab with Molly, but the scarcity of supplies forces him to make dangerous trips outside to the settlement markets, leaving Molly alone and locked away for her own safety.
