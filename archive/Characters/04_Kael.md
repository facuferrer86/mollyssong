# Kael

- **Role:** Deuteragonist. A boy from the settlements who befriends Molly.
- **Age:** Early teens.
- **Appearance:** Wiry and quick, with eyes that have seen the harshness of the Wastelands. Dressed in practical, scavenged gear.
- **Personality:** World-wise and street-smart, but with a core of idealism. He is skeptical of the old world's tech but fascinated by the stories. He represents the new generation of humanity, born into the ruins.
- **Motivation:** Initially driven by curiosity and a desire to explore the unknown. His connection with Molly makes him protective, and he becomes her anchor to the world outside the lab. He is the voice of caution against Elias's grand, dangerous plans.
