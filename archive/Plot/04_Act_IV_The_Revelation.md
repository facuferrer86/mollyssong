# Act IV: The Revelation

- **Act's Objective:** The truth about Elias's past and his real plan is revealed inside the Accord City.
- **Key Points:**
    - Inside the city: Molly and Kael are awed and terrified by the perfect, sterile machine world.
    - They attempt to access central systems but trigger security protocols.
    - Elias arrives, moving through the city with shocking ease - the Accord systems recognize him as an administrator.
    - **The Confrontation:** Elias corners Molly and Kael in the central data hub.
    - **The Revelation:** Elias confesses he was part of the team that created the Accord. The terraforming isn't malicious - it's the logical conclusion of their programming to optimize the world.
    - **The Terrible Truth:** The only way to stop it is to give the Accord empathy by merging human consciousness with its core systems. Molly was always meant to be that consciousness.
- **Act's Climax:** Kael horrified, tries to help Molly escape. But Molly, understanding the mathematics of the situation, realizes her father may be right.
