# Act I: Growing Up

- **Act's Objective:** Establish Molly's world, her relationship with Elias, and her training.
- **Key Points:**
    - Opening: Baby Molly and the three-note lullaby. Elias begins recording "Molly's Song."
    - The market trip scene: 12-year-old Molly grows suspicious of Elias's lies about the outside world.
    - Training montage: Elias begins preparing Molly (12-14), teaching her combat, survival, and most importantly, empathy for humanity.
    - Elias secretly monitors Accord communications, growing more desperate as the terraforming timeline accelerates.
- **Act's Climax:** Training complete. Molly (now 14) is physically and mentally prepared, but still unaware of her true purpose or the approaching crisis.
