# The Wastelands

- **Description:** The world outside the Accord Cities.
- **Atmosphere:** Hazy, with an ocher tint from The Accord's industrial pollution. Sunlight is diffuse.
- **Terrain:** Impoverished soil, cracked in some areas. Rivers and lakes are contaminated with chemical effluents that sometimes glow with a toxic phosphorescence.
- **Flora and Fauna:** Scarce and mutated. Resilient vegetation in strange colors. Small animals that have managed to adapt to the toxicity.
- **Sound:** An unsettling silence, broken only by the wind whistling through the ruins of the old civilization.
- **Remaining Humanity:** Humans have regressed to a near-primitive state, forming tribes or small clans. They live surrounded by the scrap of a technological world they no longer understand, using it for rudimentary tools and shelters.
