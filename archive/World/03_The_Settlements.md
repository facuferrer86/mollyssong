# The Settlements

- **Description:** Small enclaves where the last vestiges of humanity survive.
- **Location:** Generally on the outskirts of the ruins of old cities, using pre-Accord structures as a foundation.
- **Technology:** A mix of salvaged technology and improvised solutions. Mended solar panels, constantly failing water purification systems.
- **Society:** Tightly-knit communities. Social life often revolves around makeshift markets.
- **The Market:** A chaotic and dusty meeting point where scavengers trade technological scrap, hard-won food, and purified water. It is a place of barter, rumors, and occasional danger.
- **Relationship with The Accord:** They are completely ignored. To The Accord, they are like an ant's nest in a mansion's garden. Not worth the effort to eliminate.
